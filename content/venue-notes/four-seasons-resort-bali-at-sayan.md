# 峇里島薩揚四季度假酒店 (Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan)

## 快速判讀
- 地區：Ubud
- 場地型態：jungle, garden, lawn, rooftop, villa-buyout
- 價格狀態：public_price_available
- 價位帶：luxury
- 雨備強度：medium
- 住宿整合：workable_with_shuttles
- 交通風險：high
- 照片參考價值：high

## 適合誰
- Couples who want an unmistakable Ubud jungle identity instead of a beach or chapel wedding
- Intimate destination weddings where atmosphere matters more than raw guest count
- Design-led celebrations that want private-villa or lotus-pond storytelling

## 不適合誰
- Large weddings that need a ballroom-scale rain plan
- Guests who need the simplest airport transfer and flattest mobility
- Budgets that need a lower-entry Ubud wedding venue

## 主要優勢
- One of the clearest jungle-and-river visual identities in Bali
- Private-villa and lotus-pond formats feel distinctive rather than generic
- Public pricing exists, which is uncommon for ultra-luxury Ubud venues

## 主要風險
- Weather is part of the charm and also one of the biggest operational risks
- Guest transport and mobility are more complex than south-Bali beach resorts
- Capacity tops out quickly if your guest list starts growing past intimate-to-mid size
